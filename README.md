# 📊 Global E-commerce Sales Analysis using Python

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes a Global E-commerce Sales dataset using Python to uncover valuable business insights related to sales performance, profitability, customer segments, regional performance, and shipping trends. The project demonstrates the complete data analysis workflow, including data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), visualization, and business recommendations.

The objective is to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights that can support business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze sales and profit across product categories.
- Identify the top-performing regions and states.
- Understand customer segment performance.
- Discover the best-selling products.
- Analyze monthly sales trends.
- Evaluate the impact of discounts on profit.
- Analyze shipping mode preferences.
- Explore relationships among key business metrics.

---

## 📌 Key Insights

- Technology products generated the highest sales and profit.
- The West region recorded the highest sales.
- California contributed the highest revenue among all states.
- Consumer customers generated the largest share of sales.
- A small number of products contributed significantly to total revenue.
- Monthly sales exhibited seasonal fluctuations.
- Higher discounts generally reduced profitability.
- Standard Class was the most frequently used shipping mode.
- Sales and profit showed a positive correlation.
- Correlation analysis highlighted important relationships between business metrics.
